Our Services

Painting

Our experts excel in interior and exterior painting for residential and commercial properties. Meticulous attention is given to detail, from paint selection to technique, ensuring a flawless finish.

Lawn Care

Tailored to your property's size and needs, our lawn care service offers comprehensive solutions, from grass cutting to effective organic fertilization. Key practices include trimming, pruning, and weed control for a vibrant and healthy lawn.

JANITORIAL SERVICES

Our janitorial cleaning service, adaptable to any property size, ensures effective cleaning activities on a regular basis. Sweeping, restroom and sink cleaning, trash disposal, and surface dusting are part of our essential services.

Driveway Maintenance

Driveways play a crucial role in your property's image. Our services include pressure washing, grease stain removal, and restoration of concrete and paver driveways or walkways. Applying sealants and fillers in damaged areas maintains a clean appearance, saving you from costly replacements.

LAST MINUTE SERVICES

Emergencies or last-minute damages are inevitable. Our minor repair service is designed to prevent major damage. Whether it's fixing a leak, repairing walls, or upgrading fixtures, our last-minute services, led by Mario Velasquez, quickly resolve issues, providing peace of mind for property owners.
